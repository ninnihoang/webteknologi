Purpose and Goals

Audience

The target audience are big companies and startups who wants to increase their expertise within design skills, especially with interactive design, product design and service design. This includes companies or startups who need help on a project and want some inspiration and guidance by attending a workshop, or who are interested in developing a better understanding for design. Other audiences can include students who are interested to getting to know Designhjelpen and want to be a part of the team, or students who want to access information through the workshops that can help them with their school assignments.

The website gives easy access to the contact information of those owning the site, which makes it easy for the audience to email them if they have any questions, or submit an application for a workshop. They can also use the website to get access information about Designhjelpen, and the page has a simple and visually appealing design.
